Why I Love Sky:

Honestly, I can't think of a single bad thing about this game. I first found a Sky tutorial while in my youtube suggestions, and I was enthralled by the art. Next thing I know, I'm begging my mom to buy it for me... Only to find it's free!

The animation, story, and controls are as good, if not better, then other games in the MMO RPG genre. The main difference between it and other games, is that you are not competing. Many, if not all objectives, encourage you to work alongside other players, and the currency is based on friendship.
